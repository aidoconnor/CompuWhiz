About Us

Who We Are

CompuWhiz is based in Castleisland Co. Kerry. We are committed to providing customers the best and most affordable customer service in computer repairs and IT solutions.

What We Do

Our aim is to deliver the best service possible to our customers. Many other computer repair & support companies charge customers for just giving a diagnosis of the problem before they even fix it. Our motto is “No Fix, No Fee”. We have full confidence in our abilities that we can meet our customers every satisfaction.

With constant changes to technology, it can be hard to understand what hardware/software can best meet your needs. We are a company you can rely on and trust whether it is a once off fix or constant support.

Our History

CompuWhiz was set up in 2012 with the intention of delivering fast and affordable computer repair and IT services to home and small-medium sized business users. Our Team consists of experienced IT personnel with degrees in relevant IT disciplines. We provide support, sales and general IT consultancy.
